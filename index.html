The Project

It's Never Enough!
Whether or not you finish your API homework, it comes back to haunt you in other homeworks. How. Rude.
Sigh, so this project's goal was to look at whether weather is actually nicer near the Equator. One can assume it gets hotter, right? Isn't that what we learned in elementary school or something? But I was taught a lot of things in elementary school (or really, all levels of school) that were... questionable (can we say "institutionalize racism?"). So why not test this idea of better weather at the Equator?
This project sourced data from OpenWEatherMap API in conjunction with CityPy to investigate the weather at different latitudes around the world. We pulled data on city latitude and looked at the Temperature, humidity, cloudiness, and wind speed associated with each city. Our sample was around 500 cities, each with different latitudes and different weather. Come, let's take a look at the oh-so-exciting results!
